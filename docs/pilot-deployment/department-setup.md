# Department Setup

Departments drive document routing, inbox ownership, workflow assignment, and reporting.

## Steps

1. Open `/admin/departments`.
2. Create every department participating in the pilot.
3. Use clear names that match real office language.
4. Assign department heads where available.
5. Confirm users are assigned to the correct department.
6. Review dashboard and reports for department visibility.

## Pilot starter list

- Administration
- Finance
- Pastoral Office
- Programs
- Media
- Facilities

Adjust the list to match the pilot organization. Do not create unnecessary departments that staff will not use during the pilot.
